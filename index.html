About GlampTastic


Find a getaway that combines luxury and comfort with the adventure and beauty of the outdoors, all at an affordable price.


Across the UK, farmers are diversifying by setting up glampsites in their fields after harvest. For around £75 a night for the whole family you can enjoy sleeping under canvas in a real bed with sheets, rugs, soft furnishings and kitchen equipment.

No need to set up camp, pack away your tent or carry your kit… its all done for you. With wood for the campfire provided, fairy lights and heaters, you can enjoy all that’s good about camping without the hassle and discomfort.
